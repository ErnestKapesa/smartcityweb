About Smart City Solar

Leading the solar revolution in Africa and beyond

Powering Africa's Sustainable Future

Smart City Solar is a premier provider of advanced solar energy solutions, specializing in high-efficiency N-Type photovoltaic modules and cutting-edge energy storage systems. Based in Lusaka, Zambia, we are at the forefront of Africa's renewable energy transformation.

Our commitment to excellence drives us to deliver innovative, reliable, and sustainable solar technology that empowers communities, businesses, and industries across the continent and beyond.

With a focus on quality, performance, and customer satisfaction, we provide comprehensive solar solutions backed by industry-leading warranties and world-class technical support.

Our Mission

To accelerate the adoption of clean, renewable energy across Africa by providing world-class solar solutions that are accessible, reliable, and sustainable. We strive to empower communities and businesses with the technology they need to achieve energy independence and environmental stewardship.

Our Vision

To be Africa's most trusted solar energy partner, recognized for innovation, quality, and impact. We envision a future where clean energy powers every home, business, and community, creating a sustainable and prosperous continent for generations to come.
