About Smart City Solar

Smart City is a leading manufacturer of photovoltaic (PV) modules and energy storage batteries, with our state-of-the-art production base located in Wuxi, Jiangsu, China. We are committed to advancing global renewable energy solutions, specializing in high-quality solar products and integrated energy storage systems that serve customers across Europe, South America, and Africa.

With a strong international presence, Smart City has established a dedicated office in Lusaka, Zambia, to better serve the Southern African market. We have successfully delivered multiple large-scale power plant projects across Zambia and neighboring regions, demonstrating our expertise and commitment to sustainable energy solutions.
