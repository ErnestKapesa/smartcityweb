About Smart City Solar

Smart City Solar is a leading provider of advanced solar energy solutions, specializing in high-efficiency N-Type photovoltaic modules and energy storage systems. Based in Lusaka, Zambia, we are committed to delivering sustainable, reliable, and cutting-edge solar technology to power homes, businesses, and communities across Africa and beyond.

Our products feature the latest innovations in solar technology, including N-Type cells, TOPCON technology, and advanced bifacial designs, ensuring maximum energy output and long-term reliability with industry-leading warranties.
