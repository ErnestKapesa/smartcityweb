Our Technology

Innovation driving the future of solar energy

Advanced N-Type Solar Technology

Smart City Solar leverages cutting-edge N-Type photovoltaic technology to deliver superior performance, efficiency, and longevity. Our modules represent the pinnacle of solar innovation, combining advanced materials science with precision engineering.

N-Type Cell Technology

N-Type silicon cells offer superior performance compared to traditional P-Type cells, with higher efficiency, better temperature coefficients, and virtually no light-induced degradation (LID).

  • Higher efficiency ratings
  • Better low-light performance
  • Reduced degradation over time
  • Superior temperature coefficient

SMBB Technology

Super Multi Busbar (SMBB) technology uses more busbars with thinner ribbons, reducing electrical resistance and improving current collection efficiency across the cell surface.

  • Reduced electrical losses
  • Enhanced current collection
  • Better shade tolerance
  • Improved reliability

Half-Cut Cell Design

By cutting cells in half, we reduce resistive losses and improve module performance, especially in partially shaded conditions. This design also minimizes hot-spot risks.

  • Lower operating temperature
  • Reduced hot-spot risk
  • Better partial shade performance
  • Increased power output

Bifacial Technology

Our bifacial modules capture sunlight from both front and rear sides, generating up to 20% additional power from reflected and diffused light on the module's backside.

  • Up to 20% power gain
  • Dual-sided energy capture
  • Optimized for various installations
  • Enhanced energy yield
